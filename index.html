Our Features

Our innovative features

Smart Menu

Give your customers access to our smart menus that will contain relevant info. If there is a special wine, your customers should know what exactly makes it special. Also, it can help people be aware of any food allergens beforehand. Customers can place orders and customizations more accurately with the virtual menu.

Ordering system

Develop an ordering system personalized according to your restaurant needs and industry. AB test different products with different prices and get the optimum price to market and sell your products.

Real-time Changes

Delays in data can block business progress. Get data and changes in real-time as and when needed. This feature enables you with floor data, inventory notifications, and a live feed of any bottlenecks or problems.

Analytics for owners

Get the most out of all your consumer data. With FoodFormula, you will get regular reports with visual data that will help you gain a deeper understanding of where the business stands.

Membership reward system

Integrate a reward system to engage with your customers and boost loyalty. With a reward system, you create a community of your loyal customers. It will help you understand the Customer Lifetime Value.

Gamified waiting time

Waiting is the most boring thing in a restaurant. Don’t let go of this opportunity to engage with your customers. With this feature, your customers will be able to play games within your platform while they wait.
